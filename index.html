Podar International school



Podar International School is a group of schools which is a part of the Podar Education Network that was established in 1927 by Sheth Anandilal Podar, with Mahatma Gandhi as the first President of the trust. The group is headquartered in Mumbai.

Achievemnts



“Success means having the courage, the determination, and the will to become the person you believe you were meant to be.” – George Sheehan Inspired by our motto ‘Excellence in Education’, we work with each student for their overall development while maintaining the highest standards of education. Our learning approach provides a healthy and progressive environment for students to achieve excellence in academic and co-curricular activities. We nurture and prepare our students to excel in a world full of opportunities. We develop confidence, open-mindedness and the ability to recognise self-strength in our students to pursue their dreams and aspirations, wherever they may take them. This supportive environment at Podar International School has provided our students with the opportunity to take part in various Inter-School and National Events. Here are some of the student achievements, which we are proud to showcase here.

Podar International School has been recognized amongst the Top 10 International Schools in Mumbai by the Hindustan Times Annual Mumbai School Survey 2019 and Times School Survey 2019.



PIS bagged the top two position at the international round for the makex intenational robotics championship 2019

We are brimming with pride as we share with you that our students have bagged the top 2 positions at the ‘National Round’ for the MakeX International Robotics Championship. They will now represent ‘Team India’ at the International round scheduled to take place in China. Take a bow National Champions!!!! Aarav Parikh and Baseeim Siddique: 1st Position Prayrit Jain and Aarush Yadav: 2nd Position Congratulations to both the teams from the entire ‘Podar Family’. You’ve made us very proud.



Podar’s footballers won the U/14 boy’s category at the Reliance Foundation Football tournament 2019

We take pride in sharing with you that our budding footballers won their first match at the Reliance Foundation Football tournament 2019 today, in the U/14 boy’s category. With a score of 3-0, our boy’s claimed victory against the team from Bhakti Vedanta School. The goals were scored by Viraj Sood – 2 and Veer Punamiya – 1. Well Done Boys!!!
